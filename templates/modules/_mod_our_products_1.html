Coconut Coir Fiber

A coir compressed bale represents a high-quality aggregation of coconut coir fibers. These fibers are meticulously extracted, sun-dried, and carefully graded for optimal quality. The compression process, which results in a bale weighing approximately 150 kilograms, ensures density and uniformity. This superior quality bale is well-suited for horticulture, erosion control, and diverse manufacturing applications, delivering reliability and eco-friendliness due to its sustainable coconut husk origin.
