How We Work

Our Coir Production Process

1. Sourcing

We carefully select coconut husks from local farmers, ensuring quality raw materials while supporting the community.

2. Processing

Using environmentally friendly methods, we extract and process the coir fibers to maintain their natural strength.

3. Crafting

Our skilled artisans transform the processed fibers into high-quality products, combining traditional techniques with modern innovation.

4. Delivery

We carefully package and distribute our products worldwide, ensuring they reach you in perfect condition.
